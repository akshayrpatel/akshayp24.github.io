WELCOME TO BEST DRY CLEANER PALMETTO, Florida

Best Dry Cleaners is your best choice for everyday, high-fashion and couture dry cleaning services on every kind of garment in your wardrobe and more. We have more than 40 years combined dry cleaning experience. Depend on us for your dry cleaning needs in the Palmetto, Ellenton Parish, Bradenton and Terra Ceia areas. From wedding dress cleaning and preservation to alterations and even cleaning of household items, we’re here for you. We’re also pleased to provide wash-and-fold service and other speciality services to our valued clients like you.
